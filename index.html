ABOUT US

We are a small team of students formed by the Technological University of Santa Catatarina, passionate about video games who have decided to create their own world with a nostalgic retro RPG type atmosphere where we will give them incredible characters and fun-filled adventures in Invasion Drone

Level designer, Quality control (QA): Create the levels and missions of the video game. Level design establishes the physical limitations of the world. He is in charge of the quality of the game.
